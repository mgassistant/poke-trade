# Poké-Trade: How Trading Works — Video Storyboard

**Duration:** 60-90 seconds
**Style:** Screen recording + animated overlays
**Music:** Upbeat, gaming/collector vibe
**Voice:** Energetic but clear narrator

---

## Scene 1: Hook (0-5s)
**Visual:** Quick cuts of valuable Pokémon cards (Charizard, Umbreon VMAX, Pikachu VMAX)
**Text overlay:** "Trading cards should be easy, safe, and fair."
**VO:** "What if trading Pokémon cards was as simple as sending a message?"

## Scene 2: The Problem (5-12s)
**Visual:** Split screen — left shows sketchy social media DM trades, right shows overpriced marketplace fees
**Text overlay:** "No more sketchy DMs. No more 15% fees."
**VO:** "Forget risky social media trades and marketplace fees eating your profits."

## Scene 3: Introduce Poké-Trade (12-18s)
**Visual:** Poké-Trade logo animation → homepage hero
**Text overlay:** "Introducing Poké-Trade"
**VO:** "Poké-Trade is a structured trading platform built by collectors, for collectors."

## Scene 4: Step 1 — Find a Card (18-28s)
**Visual:** Screen recording of browsing marketplace → clicking on Umbreon VMAX card
**Text overlay:** "Step 1: Find what you want"
**VO:** "Browse 19,000+ cards with live pricing from TCGPlayer, eBay, and CardMarket. Found something you want? Hit 'Propose Trade.'"

## Scene 5: Step 2 — Create Offer (28-42s)
**Visual:** Animated trade offer creation — drag Charizard EX to "You Offer" + type $25 cash → Umbreon VMAX in "You Want"
**Text overlay:** "Step 2: Make your offer"
**VO:** "Select cards from your collection, add cash to balance the value, and our Fair Value Engine shows both sides if the deal is fair."
**Callout:** Green badge "✓ Fair Trade — $70 vs $85"

## Scene 6: Step 3 — Negotiate (42-52s)
**Visual:** Counter offer animation — other trader adjusts cash from $25 to $40
**Text overlay:** "Step 3: Negotiate"
**VO:** "The other trader can accept, counter, or decline. Unlimited counter offers until both sides are happy. Every version is saved."

## Scene 7: Step 4 — Ship & Verify (52-65s)
**Visual:** Trade agreed screen → upload tracking number → shipping animation
**Text overlay:** "Step 4: Ship with confidence"
**VO:** "Once agreed, upload your tracking number. Both sides can follow the shipment. Optional authentication from PSA, BGS, or CGC for high-value trades."

## Scene 8: Step 5 — Complete (65-72s)
**Visual:** "Trade Complete 🏆" celebration animation → both traders rate each other → reputation badges level up
**Text overlay:** "Step 5: Build your reputation"
**VO:** "Confirm delivery, rate your trading partner, and watch your Trader Rank grow."

## Scene 9: Trust & Safety (72-80s)
**Visual:** Icons flying in — Shield (dispute resolution), Scale (fair value), Lock (secure), Star (reputation)
**Text overlay:** "Built-in trust & safety"
**VO:** "Every trade has dispute resolution, shipping verification, and a full audit trail. Your cards, your deals, your peace of mind."

## Scene 10: CTA (80-90s)
**Visual:** Poké-Trade homepage with signup form
**Text overlay:** "Start trading free. No credit card required."
**URL:** poke-trade.com
**VO:** "Join thousands of collectors trading safely on Poké-Trade. Sign up free — no credit card required."
